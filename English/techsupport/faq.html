FAQ

· How to deal with product quality problems? >
When you receive the company's products and find quality problems that affect sales during inspection, please contact the salesman in charge of your area as soon as possible, and he will give you a processing reply in the fastest time.
· How to clean Shangri-La curtains and honeycomb curtains? >
Use the moderate wind of the handheld vacuum cleaner or the cold wind of the hair dryer to blow off the dust in the curtain first, and then wipe the upper beam and lower rod with a clean and semi-dry towel. Note: do not use hot air to avoid damage to the fabric surface.
· How to clean the sunshine fabric? >
The surface of the sunshine fabric is covered with nanoparticles. If it is stained with dirt or dust, it can be wiped off with a clean towel. If it is stained with oil, it can be cleaned with mild cleaning products, which will not affect the normal use of the fabric.
· How to get the company's latest fabric product information? >
Ways to obtain the company's latest fabric product information: 1. Salesperson contacted to provide samples; 2. The company's annual exhibition; 3. The company's official website and official account push.
· What organization does Linlongjia generally use to test fabric standards? >
The company's test reports are generally issued by authoritative organizations such as SGS Group, Jiangsu Textile and Dyestuff Quality Inspection Center, and Jiangsu Zhongzhi Fire Products Testing Center.
· Is Linlongjia's window decoration fabric safe for children? >
The company's fabrics have passed Oeko-Tex standard certification and GREENGURAD certification. Oeko-Tex Standard 100 is the most authoritative and most influential textile eco-label in the world. Oeko-Tex Standard 100 prohibits and restricts the use of known potentially harmful substances on textiles. GREENGUARD is internationally recognized as the most authoritative certification standard for indoor products (building materials, decoration materials, furniture, floor materials, surface materials, paints and coatings, cleaners and children's products, etc. more than 20 categories) (for chemical volatilization of products in indoor air) Condition). At the same time, we have also obtained the ten-ring certification of China's environment in China.
· What level of color fastness can Linlongjia fabrics reach? >
At present, the color fastness of the company's fabrics can reach (GB/T 8427-2008) Class 8.
· What flame retardant standards are currently used in the window decoration textile industry? >
The flame retardant standards commonly used in the past are: American standard flame retardant standard NFPA 701, German B1 DIN 4102, national B1 level (GB17591-2016 and GB8624-2012), among which GB8624-2012 in B1 level is higher than GB17591-2016 flame retardant standard . Seodaemun products can meet the above standards.
· What level can the flame retardant grade of Linlongjia's fabric reach? >
The sunshine fabric products of Xidamen can reach the national B1 flame retardant level GB8624-2012.
