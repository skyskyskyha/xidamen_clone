ABOUT US

Changzhou Linlongjia Textile New Materials Co., LTD., located in Wujin Economic Development Zone, Changzhou City, Jiangsu Province, is a high-tech enterprise integrating R&D, manufacturing and sales. The company has been focusing on the field of engineering curtain fabric for more than 20 years. At present, the company produces environmental protection and multi-functional engineering shading fabrics with scientific and technological content such as curtain, sun fabric and Roman curtain. The core technology is coating. Through color matching, foaming, coating, calendering and other processes, the curtain fabric has the functions of shading, flame retardant, anti-mildew, anti-bacterial, waterproof, anti-static, anti-oil, heat insulation, anti-ultraviolet and so on. The company has passed ISO9001 quality management system certification, other technologies have passed the test of the international authority, the company has several patents, a registered trademark, with independent import and export rights. The company has a professional and experienced R & D team, in fabric development, pattern design, paste allocation, coating technology and other aspects of strong R & D capabilities, the company to implement strict quality management of products, product quality and effect by customers. Innovation to create value, quality to achieve win-win is the company's core values, to provide customers with quality products is our purpose, to provide customers with quality service is our attitude, the company's products are sold all over the domestic, at the same time exported to the United States, Europe, Russia, Australia, the Middle East, Southeast Asia and other countries and regions. The company will continue to innovate and upgrade, adhering to the spirit of professionalism and focus, and sincerely hope to cooperate with you with cost-effective products and good service.
